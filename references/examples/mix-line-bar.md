# 折柱混合

**Category:** `bar`
**Official:** https://echarts.apache.org/examples/zh/editor.html?c=mix-line-bar
**Template:** examples/mix-line-bar.html
**Data Format:** `{ categories: string[], barData: number[], lineData: number[] }`
