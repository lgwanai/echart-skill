**Category:** `line`
**Official:** https://echarts.apache.org/examples/zh/editor.html?c=line-stack
**Template:** examples/line-stack.html
**Data Format:** `{ categories: string[], series: [{name: string, stack: string, data: number[]}, ...] }`
